🐓 About Dodd Poultry Fam

Welcome to Dodd Poultry Fam! My journey to founding this business has been shaped by my personal experiences, resilience, and a deep connection with animals.

My Story

Growing up, I faced bullying in middle and elementary school due to my identity as a homosexual. Those early years were tough, but they also taught me important lessons about acceptance and finding strength within myself. With the support of my family and my passion for animals, I found peace and a sense of purpose.

FFA Journey - High School

During high school, I was actively involved in the Future Farmers of America (FFA), which was a pivotal experience for me. I had the opportunity to raise various livestock, including my beloved pig, Bella, as well as chickens that qualified for auction. I also worked with a lamb named Bow, both of which I entered into sale auctions, ultimately earning accolades like grand champion chickens and participating in the heifer program.

Sophomore Year: I raised a pig named Bella and a batch of meat chickens. My chickens made the qualifications for the auction sale - my first major achievement!

Junior Year: I expanded my program, raising a pig, chickens, and a lamb named Bow. My lamb made sale auction qualifications.

Senior Year: This was my most successful year. I raised a lamb, pig, batch of chickens, and started a heifer program for students at my school using my grandfather's cattle. I earned Grand Champion with my chickens and made sale qualifications with my pig!

Starting Dodd Poultry Fam

On February 19, 2025, I officially started Dodd Poultry Fam by purchasing 14 chicks from Heiden's Feed and Supply. That first batch included one rooster, and from there, the business grew and continues to grow today.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing high-quality poultry products while building a community of customers who support local, family-owned businesses. We believe in treating our animals with respect and care, just as I learned through my FFA experience.
