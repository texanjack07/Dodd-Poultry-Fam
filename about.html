About Dodd Poultry Fam

Welcome to Dodd Poultry Fam! My journey to founding this business has been shaped by my personal experiences, resilience, and a deep connection with animals.

My Story

Growing up, I faced bullying in middle and elementary school due to my identity as a homosexual. Those early years were tough, but they also taught me important lessons about acceptance and finding strength within myself. With the support of my family and my passion for animals, I found peace and a sense of purpose.

During high school, I was actively involved in the Future Farmers of America (FFA), which was a pivotal experience for me. I had the opportunity to raise various livestock, including my beloved pig, Bella, as well as chickens that qualified for auction. I also worked with a lamb named Bow, both of which I entered into sale auctions, ultimately earning accolades like grand champion chickens and participating in the heifer program.

With all these experiences, I decided to turn my passion for animals into a business. Thus, on February 19, 2025, Dodd Poultry Fam was founded, starting with just 14 chicks sourced from Heiden's Feed and Supply. Our mission is to provide quality poultry and uphold the values of hard work and dedication that I learned throughout my journey.

Thank you for visiting our website, and I hope to share my journey with you as we grow together!
