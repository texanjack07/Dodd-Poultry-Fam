🐓 About Dodd Poultry Fam

Welcome

Welcome to Dodd Poultry Fam! We are a small family farm dedicated to raising happy, healthy poultry and bringing fresh, high-quality products to our community.

My Story

Growing up, I faced challenges with bullying in school due to my identity. Those difficult years taught me resilience and the importance of finding strength within myself. With the support of my family and my deep love for animals, I discovered my true passion — caring for livestock and building something meaningful.

My FFA Journey

During high school, I was heavily involved in FFA (Future Farmers of America). I raised pigs, chickens, lambs, and participated in the heifer program using my grandfather’s cattle. These experiences shaped who I am today and laid the foundation for Dodd Poultry Fam.

FFA Highlights

Sophomore Year

Raised my first pig named Bella and a batch of meat chickens. My chickens qualified for the auction sale — my first major achievement!

Junior Year

Expanded my program with a pig, chickens, and a lamb named Bow. My lamb also made it to the sale auction.

Senior Year

My most successful year yet. I raised a lamb, pig, chickens, and started a heifer program. I earned Grand Champion with my chickens and qualified my pig for sale.

Our Beginning

On February 19, 2025, I officially started Dodd Poultry Fam by purchasing my first 14 chicks from Heiden's Feed and Supply. From that small beginning, our farm has continued to grow with care and dedication.

Our Mission

We are committed to raising animals with respect and care, providing fresh, high-quality poultry products, and supporting our local community. Every animal is treated with kindness — just as I learned through my years in FFA.
